About Podfree

A powerful podcast and video editing tool designed to streamline your content creation workflow.

What is Podfree?

Podfree is an open-source podcast and video editor that allows you to edit content by manipulating transcripts. Delete words, restore sections, and see your changes reflected in real-time. Export your edited videos and audio with precision and ease.

Transcript-Based

Edit by selecting words in the transcript

Multi-Format

Export video and audio in multiple formats

Project Management

Organize your episodes in isolated projects

Notes Editor

Write and preview markdown notes

Open Source

Podfree is open source and available on GitHub. Contributions, bug reports, and feature requests are always welcome!
